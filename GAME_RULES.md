# Ohana Monopoly — Game Rules

## Overview

Ohana Monopoly is a team-based board game where teams compete to earn the most points by purchasing cities and collecting toll fees from other teams.

## Setup

- 2–6 teams, each starting with **20 points**
- The board has **20 spaces**: 15 cities, 4 Mini Games, and 1 START
- The admin controls the game from a separate console — no phones or logins needed from players

## How to Play

1. Teams take turns rolling **2 dice** (physical dice — the admin enters the result)
2. Your token moves clockwise around the board by the dice total (2–12 spaces)
3. After landing, the team takes an action based on the space type

## Space Types

### Cities ($, $$, $$$)

| Tier | Purchase Cost | Toll Fee |
|------|-------------|----------|
| $ (Low) | 4 pts | 8 pts |
| $$ (Mid) | 6 pts | 12 pts |
| $$$ (High) | 10 pts | 20 pts |

- **Empty city** → You may purchase it (if you have enough points)
- **Your own city** → Nothing happens
- **Another team's city** → You pay the toll fee to that team

### Mini Game

When any team lands on a Mini Game space, **all teams participate** in a mini activity.

Awards:
- 1st place: **+20 pts**
- 2nd place: **+10 pts**
- 3rd place: **+5 pts**

### START

- Every time you **pass or land on START**, you earn **+5 pts**

## Rounds

- The game runs for a set number of rounds (laps around the board)
- When a team completes all rounds, they are finished and removed from the board
- The game ends automatically when all teams finish

## Selling Towers

- You may sell your tower **only during your own turn**
- Selling refunds **half** of the original purchase cost

## Winning

The team with the **most points** at the end of the game wins!

Points come from:
- Passing START (+5 each lap)
- Mini Game awards (+20 / +10 / +5)
- Collecting toll fees from other teams
- Keeping unspent points from the start

Points are spent on:
- Purchasing cities
- Paying toll fees when landing on other teams' cities

## Tips

- Buy cities early to start collecting toll fees
- $$$ cities are expensive but earn you the most when others land on them
- Watch your points — you can go negative if you can't cover a toll fee
- Mini Games are a great equalizer — everyone gets a chance to earn big points

**Good luck and have fun!**
